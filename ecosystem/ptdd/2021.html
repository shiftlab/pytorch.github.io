PyTorch Developer Day

2021

PYTORCH DEVELOPER DAY INFORMATION

The PyTorch Developer Day is a virtual event that brings together leading researchers and developers from the Machine Learning (ML) community to join a multiple set of talks covering new software releases, ways PyTorch is being used in academia and industry, and current trends in ML development. Find all the talks below:

  • Keynotes: Learn about the innovations, the new features, updates, and release of PyTorch, and how industries are using it for production and deployment.
  • Fireside Chat: An informal and intimate conversation with two pioneers in the field of AI (and PyTorch) sharing their thoughts and vision for the future, commentary on top-of-mind trends they are seeing
  • Community Talks: PyTorch has grown thanks to our community. Hear from our members on the work being done with PyTorch.
